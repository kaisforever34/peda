# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Students** (primary): learners navigating courses, exams, voice practice, and dashboards. They are in goal-driven sessions — study, test, track progress. Need clarity, low cognitive overhead, and motivational feedback.

**Teachers** (secondary): educators creating courses, writing exams, grading submissions, managing classrooms. They are in task-completion mode — efficient workflows, clear data, minimal friction.

## Product Purpose

PEDA is an AI-powered learning platform that combines traditional course/exam management with voice-coach practice and gamified motivation (streaks, badges, leaderboards). Success means students learn effectively and teachers manage effortlessly — without the cruft of traditional LMS tools.

## Brand Personality

confident, warm, sharp

Sharp product design (Linear / Vercel clarity) softened by warm, approachable education-tone. Professional but not cold. Smart but not intimidating.

## Anti-references

- Traditional LMS platforms (Canvas, Moodle) — dense, cluttered, dated, overwhelming navigation
- Generic SaaS admin templates — no personality, no education context
- Glassmorphism / decorative blurs — never purely decorative
- Over-corporate enterprise dashboards — stiff, lifeless

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over decoration.** Every visual choice reduces cognitive load. Nothing decorative without purpose.
2. **Confident warmth.** Sharp layout + approachable tone. Linear precision meets Duolingo friendliness.
3. **Antithetical to LMS.** If it looks like a traditional education interface, redesign it.
4. **Accessible by default.** WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. No patterns that depend on color alone.
5. **Purposeful motion.** Animate transitions that aid understanding. Never bounce, never decorative.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA target (4.5:1 contrast, visible focus indicators, keyboard navigation)
- Support reduced motion preference
- Support color blindness — never convey meaning through color alone
- Readable type at all sizes, no forced small text
